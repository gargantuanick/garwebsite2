Edward Jones

CTO

Edward Jones is a dynamic leader with over 20 years of software engineering expertise, serving as CTO at Gargantua Group and Assistant Professor at Colorado Christian University. At Google, he led APIs utilized by over 10 million users, optimizing workflows to save engineers 15+ hours monthly. His tenure at Lockheed Martin showcased his mastery in crafting complex data ontologies--intricate, hierarchical knowledge structures designed to fuel powerful machine learning (ML) workloads--integrating AI, ML, ontologies, and speech recognition for UAVs. This expertise enabled the development of 100,000+ SLOC reusable platforms deployed across 12+ projects, earning two patents and multiple peer-reviewed papers as an invited keynote speaker.

In academia, Edward shapes future technologists by teaching programming, data analytics, and discrete math, mentoring students, and weaving faith into curriculum. As CTO, he leverages his ontology-driven ML proficiency to architect scalable, semantically rich data frameworks that enhance model accuracy and inference, driving transformative AI solutions. His ability to construct sophisticated ontologies ensures robust data representation, powering advanced ML workloads that deliver measurable business impact. A faith-driven visionary, Edward transforms research into purposeful code, inviting collaboration to create lasting value.
